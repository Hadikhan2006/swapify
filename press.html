About Swapify

Swapify is Pakistan's leading online platform for item swapping, dedicated to fostering a circular economy and promoting sustainable consumption. Founded in 2023, we empower individuals to exchange unused goods, reduce waste, and connect with a vibrant community.

Our mission is to make sustainable living accessible and rewarding for everyone, contributing to a greener Pakistan.
