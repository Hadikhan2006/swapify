General Questions

Everything you need to know to get started

Swapify is Pakistan's premier online platform dedicated to item swapping. We connect individuals who want to exchange their unused or pre-loved items for something they need, promoting a sustainable and circular economy.

It's simple! You list an item you want to swap, describing what you're looking for in return. Other users can then browse your item and propose a swap with their own items. Once both parties agree, you arrange a meeting to exchange items.

Yes, listing items and proposing swaps on Swapify is completely free! We aim to make sustainable living accessible to everyone.

You can swap a wide variety of items, including electronics, books, fashion, furniture, sports equipment, toys, home & garden items, and more. Please refer to our Community Guidelines for a list of prohibited items.

Account & Safety

Ensuring a secure and trustworthy swapping experience

Click on the "Account" dropdown in the navigation bar and select "Sign Up" or "My Profile". You'll be guided through a quick registration process.

Eco Points are our reward system for sustainable actions. You earn points by completing swaps, listing new items, referring friends, and more. You can redeem them for exclusive discounts and rewards. Visit our Rewards page for details.

We prioritize user safety. We encourage users to meet in public places, verify item conditions, and use our in-app chat for communication. We also have a reporting system for suspicious activity and clear Safety Guidelines.

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team immediately via the Contact Us page or by reporting the item/user. We will investigate and assist you.

Still have questions? Don't hesitate to reach out!
